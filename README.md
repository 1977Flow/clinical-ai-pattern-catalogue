# Clinical AI Pattern Catalogue

**7 Design Patterns for Ontological Fidelity in Clinical AI**

Most clinical AI systems fail not because of bad algorithms, but because they train on structurally distorted data. This catalogue documents the seven architectural patterns that determine whether a clinical AI system will survive deployment.

Each pattern is derived from peer-reviewed research spanning 22 disciplines and 12 publications.
